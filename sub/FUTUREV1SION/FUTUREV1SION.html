FUTUREV1SION

FUTUREV1SION is a project that I've wanted to make for a long time. This project takes inspiration from film for it's atmosphere, drawing you in with the first track as you follow me through a hazy night of self discovery.

GOLDENIGHT is the beginning of the journey. I slowly find myself slipping into my mind as I think back on my life, all while entranced by smoke.

SPRAYPAINT finds me falling deeper, my mind now surrounding me as I begin my journey. DREAMER takes this raw emotion and presents it in an alternate light, the opposite side of the coin, if you will.

CANVAS is a deeper look at what it means to be an artists through my eyes, with the idea that anyone can do it being quite prevelant.

Finally, we come upon INNERVISION, completing my journey as I come to terms with the things I've learned and figuring out how to use them to become better.

This project meant a lot to me, and was certainly a means of growth for myself, both as a person and an artist. I wanted to prove that I could do more than sample and spit some corny bars here and there. I wanted to show I could make art.

FUTUREV1SION is that art.
